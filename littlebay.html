Little Bay

Skills: Video production and editing, sound mixing

Little Bay is a short documentary style film that explores the connection between a person and their surroundings.

Little Bay was created as a final project for a film editing class. My gradfather often talks about the history of Little Bay. Between his memories and his own research that he has completed throughout the years, he paints a very detailed picture of what Little Bay was like. This film focuses on his personal connection to the area and what he felt as a young child.

The film was recorded with a Sony Alpha .

All video components were edited in Adobe Premiere. Sound was mixed in Adobe Audition.
